About Chastain Guitars

Welcome! My name is Paul Chastain and I am the founder and owner of Chastain Guitars. I have always been fascinated by guitar players and I have played the guitar for many years, which is what motivated me to create this company! My goal with the company is to show and sell some of my favourite guitars to people who would like to have a high quality guitar. If you need help with the hardware, you can always check out the hardware guide that I made because a lot of guitar players actually don't know what to look for when they're buying a new guitar, and I know how annoying it can be to find information on the internet.
